Benefits We Offer

Income

Total net annual salary from 14 to 16 months depending on your performance.

Review salary twice per year based on your performance and output.

Performance bonus twice per year.

Holiday bonus on 30/4, 1/5, 2/9 and personal birthday.

Allowances

Lunch allowance of 800,000 VND per month.

Parking allowance.

Fuel allowance if the distance from home to work is over 20km.

Bonus of 1,000,000 VND/month when achieving Ielts certificate > 6.5.

Title allowance if any.

Insurance and Beneficial Policies

Social insurance, public health insurance, unemployment insurance in accordance with national laws.

PVI Care insurance for individuals after 1 year of working and for relatives after 3 years of working.

Periodic health check in June every year.

Full salary maternity allowance.

Postpartum worktime-reduction for female employees having babies under 1 year old.

Paid leave of 12 days per year and bonus of 1 paid leave per 5 years of working according to the provisions of the Law.

Welfare regime such as wedding leave, wedding gifts, childbirth, sick visits,…

Training and Developing Policies

Participating in Knowledge Sharing sessions with OpenTalk, MiniTalk weekly.

100% sponsored exam fees for professional certification.

Udemy account for every office.

Onsite opportunities in Australia, USA, Japan …

Great promotion opportunities and career paths.

Working Environment

Comfortable, youthful working environment with 80% is 9x with dynamic and talented Gen Z PM and TechLeads management team.

The office is pleasant, and fully equipped with an attractive pantry with snacks, tea, coffee, and milo,…

Be equipped with modern PC + Laptop equipment.

Enjoy all of our activities such as Company Trip, Company’s Birthday, YEP, Sport/Game/Code War Contest,…

We have these clubs for you to join: Football, Table football, Music, English, media and more.
